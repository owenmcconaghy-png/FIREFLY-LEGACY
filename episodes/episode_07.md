<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Not the rhythm of four strangers navigating a situation. The other kind. The kind that doesn’t need to announce itself.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">From the outside it is exactly what it looks like. Dead. The kind of dead that happened slowly and was never reversed.</p>
