"Same Sky, Different Dust"

Episode Eight

**RUNNING**

**FIRST DRAFT**

## One — Running


---

*Deep Space — Exterior — Night*

Serenity moves through the black.

Unhurried. Small against the stars. The tail section glowing amber the
way it always has.

Then the cruiser.

It appears the way Alliance cruisers appear — not from somewhere but
simply present. As if the black made room for it.

It is not small.

--- — —


---

*Serenity — The Cockpit — Continuous*

CAS sees it first.

Of course she does.

Her hands are already moving before the proximity alert sounds. Reading
the board. Reading the cruiser. Reading the distance between them and
everything that isn't the cruiser.

The alert sounds.

> **CAS** We've got a problem.

SAFF is there in four seconds. Looks at the screen. Looks at the cruiser
filling the cockpit window.

> **SAFF** How long have they been there.
>
> **CAS** They haven't. They just were.

The cruiser's weapons lock. The sound of it travels through the ship —
felt before it's heard.

PIP's voice from the engine room.

> **PIP** *(over ship-wide)* That's a targeting lock.
>
> **SAFF** We know Pip.
>
> **PIP** Just checking.

JAYNE appears in the cockpit doorway. Looks at the cruiser. Looks at
Cas. Looks at the cruiser again.

> **JAYNE** Options.

Cas looks at the board. At the cruiser. At the distance. At everything
between them and not being here anymore.

A beat.

> **CAS** I'm out of ideas.

She looks at the cruiser.

> **CAS** Let's just hope they're stupid enough to fall for this twice.

Saff looks at her.

> **SAFF** Cas —

Cas turns Serenity around.

Full burn.

Straight at the cruiser.

--- — —


---

*Alliance Cruiser — Bridge — Continuous*

The officer at the weapons console looks at his screen.

Looks at his commanding officer.

Looks at his screen again.

> **OFFICER** Sir. The Firefly is —
>
> **COMMANDER** I can see what the Firefly is doing.

A beat.

> **OFFICER** What do we do.

The commander watches the Firefly coming at them. Full burn. Straight
line. No deviation.

He blinks.

> **COMMANDER** Evasive.

--- — —


---

*Serenity — The Cockpit — Continuous*

The cruiser moves.

Not enough.

Serenity goes through the gap between the cruiser's port side and where
the cruiser was a second ago. The proximity is not comfortable. The
sound of the cruiser's hull passing that close is not comfortable
either.

Then they're through.

Then the cruiser fires.

Two shots. The first misses. The second doesn't.

The ship shudders. Something in the engine room goes wrong in a way Pip
will be able to describe precisely and nobody else will understand.

Then they're in the black and the cruiser is behind them and getting
smaller.

Cas holds the heading.

Doesn't look back.

> **PIP** *(over ship-wide)* She's hit.
>
> **CAS** How bad.
>
> **PIP** Bad enough. Give me ten minutes.

A beat.

> **CAS** You've got five.
>
> **PIP** Then I'll need ten more after that.

Cas allows herself one breath.

Then she flies.

## Two — Damage


---

*Serenity — The Engine Room — Continuous*

The engine room is loud in a new way.

PIP knows all of Serenity's sounds. He has been cataloguing them since
day one the way his mother catalogued them before him, the way you learn
the vocabulary of something you love.

This sound is not in the catalogue.

He moves fast. Not panicking. Just moving with the specific economy of
someone who knows that panic costs time and time is what they don't
have.

He finds it.

A housing panel on the port drive coupling. Cracked through. Not
catastrophic but close enough to have a conversation about it.

He puts his hand on it.

The ship shudders under his palm.

> **PIP** I know. I know. Give me a minute.

He gets to work.

--- — —


---

*Serenity — The Cockpit — Continuous*

SAFF is at the nav console. JAYNE is in the doorway. CAS has both hands
on the controls and her eyes forward.

> **SAFF** We need to land. She can't take another hit in this state.
>
> **CAS** Agreed.
>
> **SAFF** Closest option is Hera.

A beat.

Jayne doesn't move.

> **JAYNE** Alliance garrison on Hera. Has been since the war.
>
> **SAFF** I know.
>
> **SAFF** What about Beylix. Independent salvage yard. They don't ask
> questions.
>
> **JAYNE** Last I heard Beylix salvage was running grey market parts to
> three different factions. Anyone who wants to find us buys the
> information for half a credit.
>
> **SAFF** I know that too.

A beat.

She looks at the nav console. At Hera. At Beylix. At the ship around
her.

> **SAFF** Give Pip time to patch her. Then we try Hera.

Jayne says nothing.

> **CAS** Keeping moving.

The ship hums.

The new sound is still there underneath everything else.

Pip's voice over ship-wide.

> **PIP** Twelve minutes. Maybe fifteen. Don't do anything dramatic in
> the meantime.
>
> **CAS** Define dramatic.
>
> **PIP** You know what dramatic is Cas.

Cas says nothing.

Jayne almost smiles.

Almost.

## Three — In The Black


---

*Serenity — Various — In Transit*

The ship moves.

The patch holds. The engine settles back into its usual vocabulary. Pip
stays in the engine room anyway.

He talks to the ship.

The ship talks back in the way she always does.

--- — —


---

*Serenity — The Cockpit — Later*

CAS flies.

Both hands on the controls. Eyes forward. The black going by.

SAFF comes in. Stands in the doorway. Doesn't say anything for a moment.

> **SAFF** How is she.
>
> **CAS** Holding.
>
> **SAFF** And you.

Cas looks at the stars.

> **CAS** Holding.

Saff looks at her for a moment.

Then she goes back to the cargo bay.

Cas flies.

--- — —


---

*Serenity — The Cargo Bay — Later*

JAYNE is at the table. Not eating. Not sleeping. Just sitting the way he
sits when he's working something out.

SAFF comes down the stairs. Looks at him.

Looks at the weapons locker.

Opens it. Starts going through inventory. Her hands need something.

The ship moves through the black.

Neither of them says anything.

That's fine.

--- — —

## Four — Hera


---

*Serenity — The Cockpit — Hera Approach*

Running dark. No transponder. No Cortex signature. Just the board and
the black and Hera as a set of coordinates getting slowly closer.

CAS watches the board.

Then she watches it differently.

Her hands slow on the controls.

> **CAS** Saff.

SAFF is there in seconds.

Cas points at the board. At two faint signatures at the very edge of
radar range. Running silent. Almost nothing. Almost.

> **CAS** They were there before we were.

Saff looks at the signatures. Looks at the coordinates for Hera still
ticking down on the nav display.

Looks at Jayne.

Jayne is looking at the board.

> **JAYNE** Told you about Hera.

Nobody answers that.

> **SAFF** Back us out. Same speed. Nothing lit up.

Cas reverses the heading.

Slow. Dark. Hera stays a set of coordinates on a board.

The signatures don't move.

They were never there.

--- — —


---

*Serenity — The Cockpit — After Hera*

Saff looks at Jayne.

Jayne looks at Saff.

Neither of them says anything.

Saff looks at the nav console.

At Beylix.

> **SAFF** I know.
>
> **JAYNE** I didn't say anything.
>
> **SAFF** You didn't have to.

She opens the comms.

--- — —

## Five — Beylix


---

*Serenity — The Cockpit — Beylix Approach*

The salvage yard sits in a valley between two ridge lines. From radar
range it looks exactly like what it is — a sprawl of old ships and
older parts and the kind of operation that runs on handshakes.

SAFF on the comms. Routine call. Engine trouble. Just need a day.

The yard talks back. Come on down. Bay four.

Cas takes them in slow.

Twenty metres.

Ten.

She stops.

> **SAFF** Cas.

Cas is looking at the yard. At bay four. At the shadows between the
stacked hulks around the landing area.

She can't say what it is. Just the ship under her hands. Just something
in the way the yard is sitting there.

> **CAS** Something's not right.
>
> **SAFF** We need to land.
>
> **CAS** I know.

She holds them at two metres.

The landing gear almost touching the ground.

Almost.

Then the shadows move.

Eight men. Maybe ten. Coming out from between the hulks. Armed. Moving
with the purpose of people who have been waiting and are done waiting.

> **PIP** *(over ship-wide)* Cas —

She's already pulling up.

Full burn.

One shot fired from below. Goes wide.

Then the yard is gone.

--- — —


---

*Serenity — The Cockpit — Immediately After*

Nobody says anything.

> **SAFF** How did you know.
>
> **CAS** I didn't.

A beat.

> **CAS** She did.

Saff looks at the controls.

Says nothing.

JAYNE is in the doorway.

> **JAYNE** We're out of options.

Saff looks at him.

At the specific quality of his waiting.

> **SAFF** You have coordinates.
>
> **JAYNE** I have coordinates.

Saff holds that.

Everything it means. Everything she doesn't know yet.

> **SAFF** Give them to Cas.

Jayne gives them to Cas.

Cas sets the heading.

The ship turns.

## Six — The Heading


---

*Serenity — Various — In Transit*

The heading is set.

Nobody asks where it goes.

PIP goes back to the engine room. Checks the patch. Still holding. He
puts his hand on the housing the way he always does.

The ship hums back.

CAS flies.

SAFF sits on the stairs and looks at nothing in particular for a long
time.

JAYNE is in his corner.

The black goes by.

Nobody talks.

There's nothing to say that the quiet isn't already saying.

## Seven — The Arrival


---

*Unnamed Moon — Exterior — Dawn*

The moon has no name worth remembering.

Rock. Scrub. A trading post on the flat that looks like it was built by
someone with low expectations and met them.

Serenity comes down through thin cloud. Cas brings her in slow the way
you bring in something that has been through something.

The landing gear touches.

The engine settles.

--- — —


---

*Serenity — The Cockpit — Continuous*

CAS looks at the nav display. At the coordinates. At the moon outside
the cockpit glass.

> **CAS** We're here.

A beat.

> **CAS** Now what.

JAYNE is in the doorway.

> **JAYNE** There's a salvage yard and trading post over there. They'll
> let you dock for repairs. No questions.
>
> **CAS** You've been here before.
>
> **JAYNE** Long time ago.

Cas looks at the trading post. Looks at Jayne.

Looks back at the trading post.

> **CAS** Alright.

--- — —


---

*Unnamed Moon — Trading Post — Two Days*

Two days.

Pip is under the ship for most of it. The patch gets replaced properly.
Other things that needed doing get done. He surfaces occasionally for
food and to report progress and then goes back under.

The crew trade what they can. Move carefully. Keep their heads down. The
trading post doesn't ask questions and the crew don't offer answers.

Jayne sits on the ramp a lot.

Watching the horizon.

--- — —


---

*Serenity — The Cargo Bay — Day Two*

SAFF comes down the stairs with her pack half assembled.

> **SAFF** Pip says she's ready. We should go.

JAYNE is on the ramp. Not looking at her.

> **JAYNE** Not yet.
>
> **SAFF** Jayne —
>
> **JAYNE** Not yet.

Saff looks at him.

At the horizon he's been watching.

At the specific quality of his waiting.

She puts the pack down.

Doesn't ask.

An hour passes.

Pip emerges from under the ship. Wipes his hands. Looks at Jayne on the
ramp. Looks at Saff.

Says nothing.

CAS comes down from the cockpit. Stands in the cargo bay.

They wait.

Nobody knows what they're waiting for.

Except Jayne.

--- — —


---

*Unnamed Moon — Exterior — Later*

Two figures come across the flat toward the ship.

Unhurried. Like they've done this before. Like they're coming home.

JAYNE sees them first.

He stands up from the ramp.

Doesn't say anything.

SAFF clocks him standing. Looks toward the flat.

At the two figures.

At the man in the brown coat.

At the woman walking beside him.

CAS sees the woman.

The way she walks. The way she holds herself. Like someone who has never
once in her life been caught unprepared.

Something happens in Cas's face.

PIP sees the man.

The set of him. The stubborn moral certainty. The specific way he moves
through the world like it owes him an argument.

Pip looks at Saff.

Saff is already looking at the man.

The two figures reach the ramp.

They don't stop.

They walk up.

Like they own the place.

Like they always have.

The man looks around the cargo bay. At the crew. At the ship around him.

Something crosses his face.

Then he looks at the crew.

> **MAL** Well. You all gonna stand there or are we gonna get to work.

ZOE says nothing.

She is looking at Cas.

Cut to black.
