<p style="color:#555555; font-size:13px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The Test Planet — Orbit — Approach</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">No ships.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">That’s the first thing. The approach reads clean. No Alliance cruisers. No patrol vessels. No anything. Just the planet sitting in its orbit and the quarantine markers drifting around it like a fence nobody maintained.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">And the mines.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">They are old. Decades old. The kind of old that makes them harder to read not easier — degraded transponders, irregular drift patterns, the specific unpredictability of hardware that has been sitting in vacuum for twenty years with nobody checking on it.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">CAS is at the controls.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">ZOE is in the co-pilot’s seat.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Neither of them is talking.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The rest of the crew are elsewhere on the ship, which is where Cas told them to be. Less weight shifting. Less noise. Less everything.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Just the two of them and the minefield and the planet beyond it.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Cas reads the board. Reads the drift. Makes a calculation that isn’t really a calculation — more like a feeling that she then checks against the numbers to see if the numbers agree.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">They do.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She takes them in.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Slow. Precise. The ship moving through the gaps between the mines the way a needle moves through fabric — not forcing, not hesitating. Just finding the space that was always there.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">A mine drifts past the cockpit glass. Close enough to see the corrosion on its casing.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Zoe watches it pass.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Doesn’t move.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Doesn’t look at Cas.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Another mine. Closer. Cas adjusts. Three degrees. Nothing dramatic. The ship responds before the thought is finished.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Zoe’s hand is flat on the co-pilot console.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Not gripping. Just there.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The last mine falls behind them.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The planet fills the cockpit glass.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Cas exhales. Once. Quietly.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Zoe looks at her.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">At the way she sits in that chair.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">At the hands coming off the controls now that the hard part is done.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">At Wash’s daughter.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">At her daughter.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She doesn’t say anything.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She doesn’t need to.</p>

<p style="font-size:13px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:1.0em; margin-bottom:0.0em; border-bottom: 2px solid #8B2500; padding-bottom: 0.5em;"></w:pBdr><w:spacing w:before="240" w:after="0"/></w:pPr><w:r><w:rPr><w:rFonts w:ascii="Courier New" w:cs="Courier New" w:eastAsia="Courier New" w:hAnsi="Courier New"/><w:sz w:val="20"/><w:szCs w:val="20"/></w:rPr><w:t xml:space="preserve"></p>

<p style="color:#555555; font-size:13px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The Test Planet — Surface — Continuous</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The planet doesn’t look like much from the air.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Green. Temperate. The kind of world the Alliance chose specifically because it looked like somewhere people would want to live.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">They put down in a clearing near the settlement perimeter. The landing gear touches. The engine settles.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Nobody moves for a moment.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">MAL</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">Weapons. Nobody goes anywhere alone. We stay together.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Nobody argues.</p>

<p style="color:#555555; font-size:13px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The Test Planet — The Settlement — Shortly After</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The first thing is the quiet.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Not the quiet of an empty place. The quiet of a place that stopped.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">There is a difference and everyone on the crew feels it immediately and nobody says anything about it because there are no words for it that don’t make it worse.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Buildings. Intact. A main street of sorts. Prefab Alliance construction — functional, efficient, exactly the same as every other settlement the Alliance ever planted on a rim world.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">And the people.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">At their desks. In their chairs. In doorways. In the street. Just stopped. The way you stop when something turns off the part of you that was going.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">No violence. No panic. No sign that anyone ran or fought or even understood what was happening.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Just absence.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">PIP is looking at a woman sitting at a desk in an open doorway. Her hands are still on her keyboard. There is a cup of something beside her that has long since gone cold.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">He looks at her for a long time.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Then he looks away.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">JAYNE is not looking at anything specific. He is looking at the middle distance the way soldiers look at things when they are somewhere they have been before in one form or another.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">MAL walks slowly.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">He has been somewhere like this before.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Not exactly like this. But close enough that his body knows what to do, which is nothing. Just walk. Just keep moving. Don’t let it land yet.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">ZOE is beside him.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The way she has always been beside him.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">SAFF is ahead of both of them.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Looking at the settlement. At the layout of it. At what this place was and what it became.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">They keep moving.</p>

<p style="font-size:13px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:1.0em; margin-bottom:0.0em; border-bottom: 2px solid #8B2500; padding-bottom: 0.5em;"></w:pBdr><w:spacing w:before="240" w:after="0"/></w:pPr><w:r><w:rPr><w:rFonts w:ascii="Courier New" w:cs="Courier New" w:eastAsia="Courier New" w:hAnsi="Courier New"/><w:sz w:val="20"/><w:szCs w:val="20"/></w:rPr><w:t xml:space="preserve"></p>

<p style="color:#555555; font-size:13px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The Test Planet — Research Facility — Later</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The facility is at the edge of the settlement. Larger than the other buildings. Reinforced. The kind of construction that says whatever is inside matters more than the people around it.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">PIP gets the door.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Inside: consoles. Banks of them. Server stacks floor to ceiling along the back wall. Everything dark. Everything waiting.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Pip moves through the room the way he moves through engine rooms — reading it before he touches it. His hands finding the main power junction without being told where it is.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">PIP</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">Give me two minutes.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">He gets it in ninety seconds.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The room comes up.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Screens. Data. Twenty years of research preserved in vacuum-sealed servers that were built to last and did.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The crew spreads through the room. Saff at the main terminal. Pip reading the server architecture. Mal walking the perimeter the way he walks all rooms — checking exits before anything else.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">CAS finds the monitor station.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">A grid of signals. Faint. Years old. Worker health monitors still transmitting on a frequency nobody thought to turn off.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">CAS</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">The workers were all fitted with monitors.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She pulls up the map overlay.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Most of the signals are spread across the settlement. Stationary. Have been for twenty years.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">But in the northwest.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">A concentration.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Forty, maybe fifty signals. Clustered. Moving.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Everyone looks at the map.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The moving signals.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Nobody says what they are.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">We scope it out. From a distance. We don’t engage.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She looks at Mal.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Mal looks at the map.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">At the signals.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">MAL</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">Agreed.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Jayne checks his weapon.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Says nothing.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">That’s enough.</p>

<p style="color:#555555; font-size:13px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The Test Planet — Northwest — Later</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">They find a building with a clear line of sight.</p>
