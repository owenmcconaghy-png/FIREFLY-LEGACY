## One — Long Time No See


---

*Serenity — The Cargo Bay — Continuous*

The cargo bay is very quiet.

SAFF is at the bottom of the stairs. PIP is beside her. CAS has come
down from the cockpit and is standing slightly apart from the other two
the way Cas always stands slightly apart.

JAYNE is on the ramp.

MAL is looking at the ship.

Not at the crew. At the ship. At the cargo bay. At the ceiling. At the
walls. At the specific texture of a place he has not been in twenty
years and which has not changed in any way that matters.

Something crosses his face.

Then he looks at the crew.

> **MAL** Well.

A beat.

> **MAL** You all gonna stand there or are we gonna get to work.

ZOE says nothing.

She is looking at Cas.

Cas is looking at Zoe.

Neither of them moves.

Mal looks at Jayne.

> **MAL** Jayne.
>
> **JAYNE** Mal.

A beat.

> **MAL** Long time.
>
> **JAYNE** Long time.

Mal looks at him.

> **MAL** Still working with hired hands out of bars I hear.
>
> **JAYNE** Here and there.
>
> **MAL** What was it — Brick and Thick?
>
> **JAYNE** Mick and Muck.
>
> **MAL** Right. How'd that work out.

A beat.

> **JAYNE** They didn't make it.
>
> **MAL** They never do.

He looks back at the crew.

At Saff.

> **MAL** Someone brief me.

## Two — The Briefing


---

*Serenity — The Mess Room — Later*

The nav console is pulled up in the middle of the table. The boxes are
there. Dave's files. The relay installation data. Everything laid out
the way you lay things out for someone who needs to understand fast.

SAFF stands at the head of the table.

MAL sits opposite. Arms crossed. Listening.

ZOE is at the far end. She has not sat down. She is watching Saff the
way she watches everything — taking it apart quietly, filing it away.

PIP and CAS are at the sides. JAYNE is in the doorway.

Saff talks.

She covers it all. The relay installation. The boxes. What Pip found.
Dave. The two days of analysis. What the data says. What New Wave is.
What the relay network means if someone finishes building it.

She doesn't editorialize. Doesn't explain what she thinks about it. Just
the facts, clean and in order, the way her father taught her without
knowing he was teaching her.

Mal listens without interrupting.

When she's done he is quiet for a moment.

> **MAL** The test world. The one they ran the trial on. You know which
> one.
>
> **SAFF** Dave has coordinates. It's not on any current chart.
>
> **MAL** No. It wouldn't be.

A beat.

> **MAL** The relay nodes. How many near completion.
>
> **SAFF** Eleven. All abandoned mid-build. Someone has been working to
> bring them back online.

Mal looks at the map on the console.

At the nodes.

At the pattern of them.

> **MAL** Someone's been planning this a long time.
>
> **SAFF** Yes.

Mal looks up at her.

At the way she's standing at the head of his table in his ship giving
him a briefing like she's done it a hundred times.

He doesn't say anything about that.

> **MAL** Jayne.

Jayne looks up from the doorway.

> **MAL** You called me in because of the boxes.
>
> **JAYNE** I called you in because of all of it.
>
> **MAL** You could have handled the Alliance.
>
> **JAYNE** I called you in because of all of it.

Mal holds that.

Looks at the boxes on the table.

Looks at Zoe.

Zoe is already looking at him.

The specific look. The one that means she knows exactly what he's
thinking and he'd better be careful about it.

He looks back at Saff.

> **MAL** Alright. Here's what we're going to do.

Saff looks at him.

Doesn't move.

> **SAFF** I'll tell you what we're going to do.

The table is very still.

Mal looks at her.

A long moment.

Then something shifts in his face. Not quite a smile. Just something
that was going to be an argument deciding not to be.

> **MAL** Let's hear it then.
>
> **SAFF** I'm still working on it. We have time.

A beat.

Mal looks at Jayne.

Jayne looks at the table.

Mal looks back at Saff.

Then he stands up.

> **MAL** I'm going to walk my ship.

He goes.

Zoe follows without being asked.

The way she always has.

The mess room settles.

Pip looks at Saff.

> **PIP** Still working on it.
>
> **SAFF** Pip.
>
> **PIP** Just checking.

Cas almost smiles.

Almost.

## Three — The Ship


---

*Serenity — The Engine Room — Later*

Mal stands in the engine room doorway.

Looks at the engine. At the modifications. At the things that are the
same and the things that aren't.

PIP is at the workbench. Doesn't look up.

> **MAL** How's she running.
>
> **PIP** Better than she should be given what she's been through.
>
> **MAL** You change the coolant mix.
>
> **PIP** Modern blend doesn't suit her. She prefers the old formula.

A beat.

> **MAL** Kaylee figured that out third week we had her.

Pip goes still.

Then keeps working.

> **PIP** Yeah.

Mal looks at him. Something in his face he doesn't say.

Then he moves on.

--- — —


---

*Serenity — The Cockpit — Later*

CAS is at the controls.

Mal comes in. Stands behind the pilot's seat.

Looks at the console.

At the stegosaurus.

He doesn't say anything for a moment.

> **MAL** You any good.
>
> **CAS** Good enough.
>
> **MAL** That's not what I asked.

Cas looks at the black outside.

> **CAS** Yes.

Mal looks at the stegosaurus one more time.

> **MAL** She pull left on a hard burn.
>
> **CAS** About three seconds in. Port thruster. You think about it
> before she does.

A very long beat.

Mal looks at the back of her head.

At the way she sits in that chair.

He looks away.

> **MAL** Good.

He leaves.

ZOE is in the doorway.

She watched the whole thing.

She looks at Cas.

At the back of her head.

At the way she sits in that chair.

Then she follows Mal.

--- — —


---

*Serenity — The Cargo Bay — Later*

Mal stands in the middle of the cargo bay.

Looks up at the ceiling. At the height of it.

SAFF comes down the stairs.

They look at each other.

> **MAL** She's in good shape.
>
> **SAFF** We look after her.
>
> **MAL** I can see that.

He looks at the cargo bay one more time.

At the walls. The floor. The ramp.

At everything exactly where it always was.

He nods.

Then he walks.

## Four — Old Business


---

*Serenity — The Mess Room — Later*

Three of them.

The rest of the crew have found places to be that aren't this room.

MAL has coffee. ZOE has nothing. JAYNE has what's left of something he
was eating earlier.

The mess room is quiet in the way it gets quiet when people who know
each other well have things to say and are working out the order.

> **MAL** Twenty years.
>
> **JAYNE** Give or take.
>
> **MAL** You look old.
>
> **JAYNE** You look exactly the same which is somehow worse.

Zoe says nothing.

She is looking at her hands on the table.

> **MAL** You left.
>
> **JAYNE** You went into hiding.
>
> **MAL** I had a child on the way.
>
> **JAYNE** I know.

A beat.

> **JAYNE** Didn't suit me. Sitting still.
>
> **MAL** No.

Mal looks at the table.

> **MAL** These kids.

Jayne looks at his food.

> **JAYNE** I know.
>
> **MAL** You should have called me sooner.
>
> **JAYNE** Probably.
>
> **MAL** Definitely.

Zoe looks up.

> **ZOE** You called him when it mattered.

Both of them look at her.

She looks at Jayne.

> **ZOE** That's enough.

Jayne holds that.

Nods once.

Mal looks at his coffee.

> **MAL** The girl.
>
> **ZOE** Don't.
>
> **MAL** Zoe —
>
> **ZOE** Not yet.

The quietest beat.

Mal nods.

He picks up his coffee.

> **MAL** The plan. What's your read.
>
> **JAYNE** Kid's got one. She just hasn't said it yet.
>
> **MAL** That bother you.
>
> **JAYNE** Used to bother me when you did it.

Mal almost smiles.

> **MAL** Still does.
>
> **JAYNE** Still does.

Zoe is looking at the door.

At the corridor beyond it.

At the ship.

> **ZOE** She'll have it by morning.

A beat.

> **MAL** You sound sure.
>
> **ZOE** I am.

She stands.

Walks out.

The two men sit in the mess room.

Mal looks at the door she went through.

> **JAYNE** She's not wrong.
>
> **MAL** She never is.

He finishes his coffee.

## Five — The Shuttle


---

*Serenity — Inara's Shuttle — Later*

The shuttle is exactly as it was.

The silk hangings. The low light. The particular quality of a space that
was built for someone specific and still carries them even after they're
gone.

MAL is sitting on the steps. Not in the shuttle. Just in the doorway of
it. Like he doesn't quite have the right to go in.

SAFF comes down the corridor and stops when she sees him.

A beat.

She sits on the steps beside him.

Neither of them says anything for a moment.

> **MAL** You met her.

Not a question.

> **SAFF** How do you know that.
>
> **MAL** Retired. Not dead.

Saff looks at the shuttle.

> **SAFF** She's well.
>
> **MAL** I know.
>
> **SAFF** You keep in touch.
>
> **MAL** After a fashion.

Saff looks at him.

At the careful way he said that.

Decides not to pull on it.

Mal reaches over and flicks the silk scarf out from under her tactical
vest.

> **MAL** You still got this old thing.

A beat.

> **SAFF** Always have.

He lets it go.

Looks at the shuttle.

Two people on the steps of a room that belongs to someone else.

> **MAL** The job.
>
> **SAFF** I know.
>
> **MAL** It's a bad one.
>
> **SAFF** I know that too.

He looks at her.

At the tactical vest. The silk over it. The specific way she holds
herself.

He doesn't say any of that.

> **MAL** You'll have a plan by morning.
>
> **SAFF** I already have one.
>
> **MAL** Then why didn't you say so.
>
> **SAFF** Because I wanted to see if you'd wait.

A very long beat.

Mal looks at the shuttle.

At the silk.

He almost says something.

Doesn't.

Stands up.

> **MAL** Brief me in the morning.

He goes.

Two steps down the corridor.

Stops.

> **MAL** One gorram day with her mother and she comes back like —

He gestures at the air.

> **MAL** How do they do that. Why are they always —

He stops.

Walks.

Muttering.

Saff stays on the steps.

Looks into the shuttle.

At the silk hangings moving in the recycled air.

She almost smiles.

Almost.

## Six — Zoe and Cas


---

*Serenity — The Cockpit — Night*

The ship is quiet.

Running lights only. The black outside going on in every direction.

CAS is in the pilot's chair.

The stegosaurus on the console.

The data chip beside it.

Cas picks it up. Turns it over. Sets it down.

Picks it up again.

ZOE comes in.

She doesn't announce herself. She just stands in the doorway. Takes the
room in.

Her eyes go to the console.

To the stegosaurus.

She looks at it for a long moment.

Then she sits in the co-pilot's seat.

The seat she used to sit in.

Cas sets the chip down.

Doesn't put it away.

Just sets it down.

The ship hums.

Neither of them says anything for a while.

Zoe looks at the board. At the display arrangement. The shortcuts. The
small adjustments.

At the way Cas sits in that chair.

The specific quality of someone completely at home in a place they have
never been before.

Zoe recognises it.

She would.

Cas turns the chip over in her fingers.

Doesn't look at it.

Doesn't put it down.

> **ZOE** You're flying her differently than when we came in.
>
> **CAS** She told me how she wanted to go.

Zoe is quiet.

Cas sets the chip on the console.

Beside the stegosaurus.

Zoe looks at it.

At the chip. At the stegosaurus. At the chip again.

She knows what it is.

Of course she does.

She looks at the black outside.

Cas looks at the black outside.

The ship moves through it.

A very long beat.

> **ZOE** Emma.

Cas goes still.

The kind of still that isn't stillness. Every muscle deciding at once
not to move.

A beat.

> **CAS** Yeah.

Zoe puts her hand on the co-pilot console.

Flat. Palm down.

Not checking anything.

Just her hand there.

Just for a moment.

She takes it back.

Stands.

At the door she stops.

> **ZOE** He would have liked this.

She goes.

Cas doesn't turn around.

Looks at the stegosaurus.

At the chip beside it.

At the black outside.

The ship hums.

It sounds, very slightly, like an answer.

## Seven — The Plan


---

*Serenity — The Mess Room — Morning*

Everyone.

First time all of them in the same room at the same time.

SAFF has the relay map up. The eleven nodes. The test world coordinates.
Everything Dave found.

The room is quiet after she finishes.

> **CAS** So we walk away.

Nobody jumps on it.

Nobody says no.

PIP looks at the map. At the nodes. At the space between them.

> **PIP** The nodes are just sitting there. Abandoned. Nobody's trying
> to bring them online.
>
> **SAFF** Not yet.

Another beat.

> **CAS** So we walk away. We don't touch the network. Nobody knows we
> know. We just... go.

She's not arguing for it.

Just laying it out.

Seeing if it holds.

MAL looks at ZOE.

Zoe is looking at the map.

At the test world.

At what she knows about test worlds.

> **ZOE** Miranda had a test world.

The room goes very still.

JAYNE puts his fork down.

Doesn't say anything.

Mal looks at the map.

At the relay network.

At what it's designed to do.

At what happens if someone finishes the job.

A long beat.

> **MAL** We can't walk away.

Nobody argues.

Not even Cas.

Pip looks at the test world coordinate.

> **PIP** We don't know how bad it is. The research is on that planet.
> Could be the whole thing died off. Could be it's dormant. Could be
> there's nothing left to activate.
>
> **SAFF** Or it could be worse than Miranda.
>
> **PIP** Or that.

A beat.

> **SAFF** We go to the planet. We find out what we're actually dealing
> with. If the research is gone or dead we pull the relay network apart
> and we walk away clean.
>
> **MAL** And if it's not gone.

Saff looks at him.

> **SAFF** Then we work out what to do about it.

Mal looks at Zoe.

Zoe is looking at him.

The specific look.

The one that means: you know what this is.

He does.

He looks back at Saff.

> **MAL** Alright.

He sits back.

> **MAL** We go to the planet.

Cas is already standing.

Already thinking about the heading.

> **CAS** I'll need coordinates.
>
> **SAFF** You've got them.

Cas goes.

Pip looks at Jayne.

> **PIP** Have you ever...
>
> **JAYNE** No.
>
> **PIP** Right.

Pip picks up his coffee.

Drinks it.

The map glows on the console.

The test world sitting there.

Patient.

*Deep Space — Exterior*

Serenity turns.

Her tail section blooms gold.

Full burn.

She goes.

**Post Credits**


---

*Alliance Command Center — Night*

The Operator's desk.

The screens.

The files.

He is looking at the last known position.

At the trail.

At where it stops.

He looks at it for a long time.

No Cortex ping.

No registry movement.

No signal of any kind.

The ship dropped off everything.

He doesn't know where they are.

He doesn't know what they're doing.

This is a new feeling.

He picks up his stylus.

Makes a note.

Underlines something.

Looks at the screen again.

At the nothing where they used to be.

He doesn't pick up the channel.

He just waits.

The screens glow.

The nothing continues.

Cut to black.
