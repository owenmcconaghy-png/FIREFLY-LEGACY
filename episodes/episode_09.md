<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">ZOE is at the far end. She has not sat down. She is watching Saff the way she watches everything — taking it apart quietly, filing it away.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She doesn’t editorialize. Doesn’t explain what she thinks about it. Just the facts, clean and in order, the way her father taught her without knowing he was teaching her.</p>
