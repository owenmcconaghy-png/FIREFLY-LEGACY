<p style="color:#555555; font-size:16px; font-weight:bold; text-align:center; margin-top:1.5em; margin-bottom:1.5em; ">One — Setting Course</p>

<p style="color:#555555; font-size:13px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:0.83em; margin-bottom:0.25em; ">The Ship — The Mess Room — Morning</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">CAS is already there when SAFF comes in.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She has the same coffee, the same chair, the same hands wrapped around the mug. But she watches her come in differently than she watched her come in yesterday. Something about the way she moves through the doorway. More purpose in it than usual, which for Saff Reynolds is saying something.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She pours coffee. Sits down. Puts both hands flat on the table.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Doesn’t drink.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">I need a heading.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Cas looks at her over her mug.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">CAS</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">Where.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She tells her. A planet. A location within it. Specific enough that she knows she’s had this ready for a while.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">CAS</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">How long.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">Three days. Maybe four if she needs it easy.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She nods. Sets her mug down. Gets up.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">CAS</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">I’ll set it now.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">He doesn’t ask why. He doesn’t ask what’s there. He just goes.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Saff watches her go. Picks up her coffee. Drinks.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The ship hums. Somewhere forward Cas is already at the helm, already finding the heading, already giving Serenity something to point at.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The mess room is quiet.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Then JAYNE walks in.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">He has a plate and he has the hat and he has the specific expression of a man whose morning routine has been entirely satisfactory up to this point. He sits in his corner. Loads his fork.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Looks at the heading on the small nav display set into the mess room wall that nobody ever looks at because nobody needs to.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">He looks at it for a moment.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Sets his fork down.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">JAYNE</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">What in the gorramn hell are we doing headed there.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Saff wraps both hands around her mug.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">Guild planet.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">JAYNE</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">I know it’s a Guild planet. Why are we going to a Guild planet.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">I need to find someone.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">JAYNE</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">On a Guild planet.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">On a Guild planet.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Jayne picks up his fork. Sets it down again. The plate gets pushed aside. This is a conversation that requires both hands free.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">JAYNE</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">Who.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">A beat.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">My mother.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The mess room is very quiet.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Jayne looks at her. At the silk scarf around her neck. At the specific quality of stillness she has that he has been recognising for a while without being able to name it.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">He names it now.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">JAYNE  (quietly, not quite a question)</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">Inara Serra is on a Guild planet.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">High Priestess of House Madrassa.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Jayne is very still.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">JAYNE  (very quietly)</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">You’re a stupid girl.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">I’ve been called worse.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">JAYNE</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">The Guild is not a place you walk into. You understand that. People who walk into the Guild without an invitation walk out changed or they don’t walk out. They trade in leverage the way —</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">He stops himself. Something crossing his face.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">JAYNE</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">They know about Mal. They know about all of it. They’ve got Alliance connections going back thirty years and they have been sitting on everything they know about that ship and everyone who ever flew on her and if you walk in there and announce yourself as Mal Reynolds’ daughter —</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">I know.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">JAYNE</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">Do you.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF  (quietly)</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">I know how to walk out of one.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She doesn’t elaborate. Doesn’t explain.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Jayne looks at her for a long moment.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">JAYNE</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">I want no part of this.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">Nobody needs a part of it.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She stands up. Picks up her mug. Looks at him.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">I’m taking the shuttle. I’ll be back.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She walks out.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Jayne sits in his corner with his pushed-aside plate and his cold coffee and the nav display on the wall showing a heading he doesn’t like.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">He sits there for a long time.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Then he picks up his fork.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Eats.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Doesn’t taste it.</p>

<p style="color:#555555; font-size:16px; font-weight:bold; text-align:center; margin-top:1.5em; margin-bottom:1.5em; ">Two — Sihnon</p>

<p style="color:#555555; font-size:13px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:0.83em; margin-bottom:0.25em; ">Deep Space — Exterior — Approach to Sihnon</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The planet comes up on sensors the way Core worlds always come up. Almost too bright. Too deliberate. The kind of world that has been shaped and tended by centuries of human intention until it glows.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Sihnon.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Blue and white and vast. Nothing like the brown-grey rim worlds they have been living in. Nothing like rock and dust and stations that smell of machine oil. This planet is polished. This planet knows it.</p>


<hr style="border:none; border-top:1px solid #8B2500; margin:2em 0;">

<p style="color:#555555; font-size:13px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:0.83em; margin-bottom:0.25em; ">The Ship — The Cockpit — Same Time</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">CAS sits at the helm looking at Sihnon through the cockpit glass. PIP leans in the doorway behind her.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Neither of them says anything for a moment.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">PIP</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">Bit different.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">CAS</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">Bit.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">They look at it a while longer.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">PIP  (to the planet, not really asking)</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">Wonder what she wants with a Core world.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">A beat.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">CAS</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">That’s her business.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Pip nods. Puts the piece of hardware back in his pocket.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">CAS</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">Shuttle’s ready.</p>


<hr style="border:none; border-top:1px solid #8B2500; margin:2em 0;">

<p style="color:#555555; font-size:13px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:0.83em; margin-bottom:0.25em; ">The Ship — Shuttle Dock — Minutes Later</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">SAFF moves through the hatch with the particular economy of someone who has done the thing they’ve been preparing to do and is now simply doing it.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">No ceremony. No hesitation.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She is wearing the silk scarf.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She always wears the silk scarf. But today it is arranged differently — not loose over the tactical vest but properly. The way it was meant to be worn. The way someone taught her to wear it before she decided she didn’t want to be taught.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The hatch closes behind her.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The shuttle undocks.</p>


<hr style="border:none; border-top:1px solid #8B2500; margin:2em 0;">

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She sets everything on the low table and crosses to the wardrobe.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The robe comes out first. Dark. Simple. Not her robes of office — something older, something that predates the title. She dresses in it without hurrying. Each fastening done correctly the first time.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Then the armour. Fitted leather, shaped to her body over years of adjustment, worn over the robe. It goes on in a specific order that her hands remember without being asked. Each piece settled before the next one begins.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The sword harness last. The two scabbards stack on her left hip. She sheathes both blades.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The quiver goes on last. Right side. Tall. The arrows separated with purpose, each one placed deliberately. Nothing casual. Nothing wasted.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She picks up the bow.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Looks at herself once in the mirror.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Not the High Priestess.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She doesn’t look back when she leaves.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The door closes.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The room is exactly as it was.</p>

<p style="color:#555555; font-size:16px; font-weight:bold; text-align:center; margin-top:1.5em; margin-bottom:1.5em; ">Six — There’s No Time</p>

<p style="color:#555555; font-size:13px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:0.83em; margin-bottom:0.25em; ">House Madrassa — The Cell — Later</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The cell hasn’t changed. The bench. The high narrow window. The door with its serious lock.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">SAFF is exactly where she was. Sitting. Waiting. She has been through everything she knows three times now and the fourth pass isn’t going to tell her anything new.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The lock turns.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The door opens.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Not guards.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">INARA.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She is standing in the doorway in a dark robe with armour over it and a bow in her left hand and a quiver on her right side and twenty years of distance between her and the daughter she is looking at for the first time.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">One second. Maybe two.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">INARA</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">There’s no time.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Her right hand goes to the quiver. Three arrows. Three draws. Her left hand barely moves.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Three guards. Three seconds. None of them reach their weapons.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The corridor is quiet.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Inara lowers the bow.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Looks at Saff.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">INARA  (moving)</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">Now there’s no time.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Saff follows.</p>

<p style="color:#555555; font-size:16px; font-weight:bold; text-align:center; margin-top:1.5em; margin-bottom:1.5em; ">Seven — The Corridor</p>

<p style="color:#555555; font-size:13px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:0.83em; margin-bottom:0.25em; ">House Madrassa — The Corridors — Continuous</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The next corridor is narrower.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Inara clocks it in the same instant she clocks the two guards coming from the far end. She drops the bow without breaking stride.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Both hands find the hilts. The blades come free in a single sweeping arc — fluid, unhurried, like the beginning of something rather than the end.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The first guard doesn’t understand what has happened until it is already over.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The second understands. Steps back. Raises his weapon.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Inara reverses the upper blade and holds it out to her left without looking.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">INARA</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">Take it.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Saff takes it.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The second guard looks at two women with blades in a corridor that has become very small and makes a decision.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">He steps aside.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">They move.</p>

<p style="color:#555555; font-size:13px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:0.83em; margin-bottom:0.25em; ">House Madrassa — The Central Hall — Continuous</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The corridor opens into something larger.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The Hall is high-ceilinged, stone-floored, the kind of space that was built to impress and currently contains approximately a dozen of Yusara’s supporters in various states of readiness. YUSARA stands at the far end. She has had time to assemble her people. She was expecting this.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Inara and Saff come through the archway and stop.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The arithmetic is obvious. Two against twelve. Yusara almost looks apologetic.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">YUSARA</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">You’ve always been remarkable, Inara. But this is —</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The door on the left opens.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">And the door on the right.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Inara’s people come through both simultaneously. Not students. Not junior Companions. People who have been in this house longer than Yusara’s faction and have been waiting for exactly this instruction. They fill the room in the specific way that changes the arithmetic entirely.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Yusara looks at the room.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Looks at Inara.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Looks at the room again.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She is too intelligent to die today over odds that have just reversed themselves. She lowers her hand. Her people follow.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The Hall is very quiet.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">INARA  (to Yusara, passing her without stopping)</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">This conversation isn’t finished.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Yusara says nothing.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Inara and Saff walk out.</p>


<hr style="border:none; border-top:1px solid #8B2500; margin:2em 0;">

<p style="color:#555555; font-size:16px; font-weight:bold; text-align:center; margin-top:1.5em; margin-bottom:1.5em; ">Eight — Mal</p>

<p style="color:#555555; font-size:13px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:0.83em; margin-bottom:0.25em; ">House Madrassa — The Outer Garden — Moments Later</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">They come out through a side gate into the lower garden. Away from the hall. Away from Yusara’s people. The incense smell is fainter here. The air cooler. The sound of water from somewhere below.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">They stop.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">For a moment neither of them says anything. The fight is behind them and ahead of them is everything else and neither of them knows how to start.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Inara looks at her daughter. At the silk scarf. At the tactical vest underneath the armour she is holding. At the specific way she stands — weight forward, ready, the way Mal stands.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She asks about the training. Not because she doesn’t know — she has known, from a careful distance, for years. But it was the last moment she had a fixed point. The last time she knew exactly where Saff was in the verse.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">INARA</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">The training. How far did you get.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">Far enough to walk out.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Inara nods. Something in her face settles.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">A beat.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">INARA</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">Mal.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Just his name. Everything it carries.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">He’s fine.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Inara nods again. Looks at her hands for a moment. Then back up.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">They talked for a while after that, in the way that people talk when twenty years is too much to cover and they’ve both decided to start from where they are instead. Practical things mostly. The verse. The ship. What Saff has been doing. Inara asking questions and Saff answering them in minimum words and both of them understanding that the minimum words are carrying a great deal more than they appear to.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">At some point Inara almost smiles.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">At some point Saff almost lets her.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Neither of them names what this is. They don’t need to.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Then Inara looks up at the sky above the garden. At the quality of the light. At something she is calculating.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">INARA</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">You need to go.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">I know.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">INARA</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">That ship cannot be in Core space. Not that ship. If the wrong person sees her —</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">I know.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Inara looks at her for a moment. Then she reaches into her robe and produces a small card. Sets it in Saff’s hand.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">INARA</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">New Melbourne. There’s a public wave board. When you see a listing for —</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She says the name of the listing. We don’t hear it. Just Saff’s face as she commits it.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">INARA</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">PO Box 1167. That’s me.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">They look at each other.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">There is no embrace. There is the specific charged stillness of two people who are standing close enough and neither of them moves and both of them know why and neither of them says so.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Then Saff turns and walks back toward the landing berth.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Inara watches her go.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">At the gate Saff stops. Doesn’t turn around.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF  (quietly, to the gate)</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">He misses you.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She walks through it.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Inara stands in the garden for a moment after she’s gone.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Then she turns and walks back into the house. She has a great deal to manage.</p>

<p style="color:#555555; font-size:16px; font-weight:bold; text-align:center; margin-top:1.5em; margin-bottom:1.5em; ">Nine — She Just Had Something To Do</p>

<p style="color:#555555; font-size:13px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:0.83em; margin-bottom:0.25em; ">The Ship — Cargo Bay — Later</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The shuttle redocks with a sound that makes everyone in the cargo bay exhale without meaning to.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">CAS is already there. PIP is already there. JAYNE is already there, arms crossed, hat on, the specific expression of a man who has been furious for several hours and has decided what he thinks about how this ended.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The hatch opens.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She reaches the bottom of the ramp. Looks at the three of them.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">Thank you for waiting.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">I just had something to do.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Jayne looks at her for a long moment. At the sword. At the cut on her arm. At the scarf.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">He doesn’t say anything.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">He turns and walks back inside.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Pip looks at the sword. Looks at her arm. Opens his mouth.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">I’m fine.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">PIP</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">I have a med kit.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">I’m fine, Pip.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Pip closes his mouth. Then opens it again.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">PIP</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">The med kit is very good.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Cas is already moving toward the cockpit.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">CAS  (over her shoulder)</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">We need to go.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Saff follows.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Pip follows Saff, med kit already in hand.</p>


<hr style="border:none; border-top:1px solid #8B2500; margin:2em 0;">

<p style="color:#555555; font-size:16px; font-weight:bold; text-align:center; margin-top:1.5em; margin-bottom:1.5em; ">Ten — Full Burn</p>

<p style="color:#555555; font-size:13px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:0.83em; margin-bottom:0.25em; ">The Ship — The Cockpit — Continuous</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">CAS drops into the pilot’s chair and her hands are already moving across the board.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">On the long range sensors, large and slow and entirely confident in its authority, an Alliance cruiser sits between them and open space. It has been sitting there for a while. It has been watching the Serenity shuttle come and go. It has been patient.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Its patience has run out.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The comms crackle.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">ALLIANCE COMMS</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">Unregistered Firefly-class vessel. You are in restricted Core space. Cut your engines and prepare to be boarded.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Cas looks at the cruiser on the sensor display. At its size. At its heading. At the specific geometry of a very large ship that has been sitting still for a long time and cannot change that quickly.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She sets their heading.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Straight at the cruiser.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF  (from the doorway)</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">Cas.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">CAS</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">I see it.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">SAFF</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">You’re pointed at an Alliance cruiser.</p>

<p style="margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0; text-align:left;"><span style="color:#8B2500; font-weight:bold;">CAS</span></p>
<p style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom:0.5em; text-align:left;">I know.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The cruiser fills the cockpit glass as Serenity accelerates toward it. On the Alliance bridge someone is running the arithmetic. A Firefly isn’t a threat. Except this one is coming straight at them and isn’t slowing down and the officer has approximately eight seconds.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Seven.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Six.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The cruiser begins to turn.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">There it is.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The moment it commits to the turn Cas cuts the pulse drive. The ship goes quiet for two seconds — just thrusters, just reaction drive, Serenity pivoting on her own axis with the specific ease of a ship that has been doing this for thirty years and knows exactly how.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Ten seconds. Maybe less.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">New heading. Away from the cruiser. Into open space. Cas hits full burn.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The tail section blooms gold.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The gap between them opens.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The cruiser is still turning when Serenity clears Core space and the black opens up ahead of them and the stars are the same stars they have always been and the ship hums and the gold fades and she moves.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Cas sits back.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Lets out a long slow breath.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Looks at the stegosaurus on the console.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Says nothing.</p>

<p style="color:#555555; font-size:13px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:0.83em; margin-bottom:0.25em; ">Deep Space — Exterior</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">The ship small against the stars. The gold already fading from her tail. The black taking her back.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She moves.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">She always moves.</p>
