# Contributing to Firefly: Legacy

Welcome. If you're here you probably love Firefly. That's enough to start.

---

## What This Project Is

A fan fiction continuation of the Firefly/Serenity universe. Ten episodes of Season One are complete. What comes next is open.

Season Two is unwritten. Individual scenes are open to development. Illustration, scoring, translation, adaptation — all of it is welcome. The only requirement is that it fits the world we've built.

---

## What's Locked

These things are established and cannot be changed:

**Canon sources:** Original Firefly series, *Serenity* (2005), *The Shepherd's Tale*, *Float Out*, *Leaves on the Wind*. The BOOM Studios All-New Firefly comics are non-canon to this project.

**The crew:**
- Saff Reynolds — 22 — Captain — Mal and Inara's daughter
- Cas Washburne — 23 — Pilot — she/her — Zoe and Wash's daughter — goes by Cas, Zoe always calls her Emma
- Pip Tam — 22 — Mechanic — Simon and Kaylee's son
- Jayne Cobb — 61 — The anchor

**The story date:** 2542, approximately twenty-four years after the film.

**The twenty-two year gap:** Fully documented in the [Context Document](docs/context_document.md). Do not contradict it.

**Season One events:** Episodes 01–10 are locked. Do not retcon them.

**Key canon facts:**
- Mal calls Cas by name for the first time in Episode 9. Before that: Emma.
- Zoe always calls her Emma. Right through to the end.
- The brown coat goes to Saff in Episode 10.
- River Tam has been managing the test planet alone for years.
- New Wave: two components, already seeded, not yet activated.
- Jayne called Mal when it mattered.

## The Writing Format

Season One uses a specific prose fiction format. If you're writing episodes, match it.

**Voice:** Nobody makes speeches. Emotional moments are oblique and sideways. Jayne never explains himself. The ship is always a character. Clipped, dry, practical dialogue in the Firefly register. Short sentences. If in doubt, less is more.

**Format rules:**
- Prose fiction, not screenplay
- Short paragraphs with breathing room
- Bold character name, dialogue on the line below
- Scene directions in italics
- Scene separators: `---`
- Em dashes (—) throughout. Not hyphens.

**What to avoid:**
- Writer's notes disguised as prose ("She files that away for later")
- Dialogue that explains the moment instead of carrying it
- Speeches
- Adjectives that do the work emotions should do

Read the episodes before writing. The voice is in them.

## How to Contribute

**Writing:**
1. Fork the repo
2. Write your episode or scene in the established format
3. Check it against the Canon section above
4. Submit a pull request with a brief note on what you've written and where it fits

**Everything else** (art, score, translation, adaptation):
1. Fork or clone the repo
2. Add your work to the appropriate folder (`/art`, `/score`, `/translations`)
3. Submit a pull request

**Found an error:**
Open an issue. Be specific — episode number, scene, the problem.
