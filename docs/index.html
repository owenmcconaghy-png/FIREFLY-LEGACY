Firefly: Legacy  —  Season One

Same Sky,
Different Dust

Still flying.

Fan Fiction  ·  Set in 2542  ·  Not for Commercial Distribution

Firefly deserved more. This is more.

Twenty-four years after Miranda. The Alliance weakened but not broken. The original crew scattered. The ship in hiding.

Three kids who don't know what they're flying. One old man who knows exactly what it is. And a ship that has been waiting a very long time for exactly this.
