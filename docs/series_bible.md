<p style="font-size:24px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:2.0em; margin-bottom:0.5em; border-bottom: 2px solid #8B2500; padding-bottom: 0.5em;">The Pitch</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.25em; margin-bottom:0.42em; ">Format: 10-Episode Season — Approximately 15-20 minutes per episode — Stylised 3D Animation</p>

<p style="font-size:16px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.5em; margin-bottom:0.5em; border-bottom: 2px solid #8B2500; padding-bottom: 0.5em;">Twenty years after the events of Serenity, the children of the original crew — led by a grumpy, ageing Jayne Cobb — must pilot a rusted-out legend to stop a new, evolved Reaver threat that the Alliance is trying to bury.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.25em; margin-bottom:0.42em; ">The official animated revival announced in March 2026 is a prequel. This project takes the opposite road. A next-generation sequel with genuine stakes, real consequences, and a story that moves the Verse forward rather than putting it in a museum.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.25em; margin-bottom:0.42em; ">Every episode earns its place on two levels simultaneously — as a standalone story and as a piece of a larger season. Someone who has never seen Firefly can follow every beat. Someone who has seen every episode of the original will feel every ghost.</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.25em; margin-bottom:0.42em; ">Ten episodes. Roughly three hours of total runtime. A complete story. Beginning, middle, end.</p>

<p style="font-size:24px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:2.0em; margin-bottom:0.5em; border-bottom: 2px solid #8B2500; padding-bottom: 0.5em;">Season Arc</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.25em; margin-bottom:0.42em; ">The season moves through four acts: the accidental crew, the growing threat, the convergence of legacies, and the passing of the torch. By the finale the kids are not the children of heroes. They are heroes. The coat confirms what was already true.</p>

<p style="color:#8B2500; font-size:19px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:1.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Pip Tam — Mechanic — Age 22</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.25em; margin-bottom:0.42em; ">Son of Kaylee Frye and Simon Tam. Pure Kaylee warmth in public. <p style="color:#8B2500; font-size:19px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:1.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Jayne Cobb — The Anchor — Age 61</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.25em; margin-bottom:0.42em; ">Post credits: The Operator finds Jayne’s file. Knows that face. Can’t place it. ‘Where do I know you from.’</p>

<p style="color:#8B2500; font-size:19px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:1.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Episode 03 — TBC — IN DEVELOPMENT</p>

<p style="color:#8B2500; font-size:19px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:1.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Episode 06 — TBC — SCAFFOLDED</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.25em; margin-bottom:0.42em; ">Someone gets hurt. Jayne knows a doctor. The doctor is Simon Tam. Kaylee is there. Simon said different.’</p>

<p style="color:#8B2500; font-size:19px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:1.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Episode 07 — TBC — SCAFFOLDED</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.25em; margin-bottom:0.42em; ">Book’s Abbey. The hidden Alliance clearance files that prove the original crew were part of something much larger than a smuggling operation. And the black box finally opens. What’s inside is the New Wave programme — not a reworked Pax formula but a fundamental redesign. Two components: an inert compound seeded into rim world water processing for years, activated by a broadcast frequency transmitted through the relay network. The body produces its own response. Off switch exists. Turn it up: compliance. Turn it higher: something else. The network is already built. The populations are already seeded. It just hasn’t been switched on. The worst thing they ever did. And it almost worked.</p>

<p style="color:#8B2500; font-size:19px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:1.33em; margin-bottom:0.33em; ">Episode 10 — THE GHOST IN THE MACHINE — SCAFFOLDED</p>

<p style="font-size:24px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:2.0em; margin-bottom:0.5em; border-bottom: 2px solid #8B2500; padding-bottom: 0.5em;">Key Themes</p>

<p style="color:#8B2500; font-size:13px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:0.75em; margin-bottom:0.17em; ">Legacy vs Identity</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.25em; margin-bottom:0.42em; ">Can the kids be heroes without just being the kids of heroes? They are running away from their legacy as much as running towards it. By the time they face the Reavers they have stopped being the Children of Serenity and become the Crew of Serenity.</p>

<p style="color:#8B2500; font-size:13px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:0.75em; margin-bottom:0.17em; ">The Truth of History</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.25em; margin-bottom:0.42em; ">Jayne knows what really happened. The sanitised versions the kids grew up with are missing the parts that hurt. The truth comes out piece by piece across the season, never as a speech, always sideways.</p>

<p style="color:#8B2500; font-size:13px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:0.75em; margin-bottom:0.17em; ">The Industrial Grit</p>

<p style="font-size:15px; font-weight:normal; text-align:left; margin-top:0.25em; margin-bottom:0.42em; ">The Verse hasn’t improved. The Alliance is still winning on paper and losing in the black. Serenity should look like a ship that has survived, not a ship that has been restored.</p>

<p style="color:#8B2500; font-size:13px; font-weight:bold; text-align:left; margin-top:0.75em; margin-bottom:0.17em; ">The Original Cast Strategy</p>
